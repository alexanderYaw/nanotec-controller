Error Balancing vs. Least Squares

Visualizing the silent precision loss on short-arc data.

Flawed: Balancing Errors (Σfᵢ = 0)

Net signed error is zero, but the centre is completely unbounded.
Result: A terrible fit (nearly straight line).

Correct: Least Squares (Σfᵢ² → min)

Uses the Kåsa method with centroid decoupling.
Result: A unique, stable, and accurate fit.
